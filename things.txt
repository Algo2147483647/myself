# Basic Things

Infrastructure: 
    Material: 
        Oxygen O2: Respiration. 
        Water H2O: Hydration.
        Salt NaCl: Electrolyte balance.
        Glucose C6H12O6 (Sucrose C12H22O11): Energy source.
        Proteins NH2-CHR-COOH: Enzymes, Structural components.
        Lipids: Long-term energy storage.
        Vitamins: Cofactors for enzymatic reactions.   
    Energy: 
        Electricity (+ electric wire, + Battery): General energy. Electric wire for transmission, Battery for storage.
        Oil: Fuel for combustion engines.
    Information, Communication: 
        Computer, Mobile Phone: Transmit, Process and Reserve information.
        Light: Produce visual information surrounding environment.
Cooking: 
    Temperature: 
        Induction Cooker: Heat food using electromagnetic fields.
        Micro-wave Oven: Heat food using micro-waves.
        Refrigerator: Cold food using vapor-compression refrigeration cycle.
    Tool: 
        Knife (+ Cutting Board): Cut large objects into smaller pieces.
        Spoon: Pick up small pieces of objects.
        Fork / Chopsticks: Pick up linear objects.
        Grinder: Grind solid objects into smaller powder.
    Container:
        Stainless Steel Pressure Cooker (+ Steaming Rack): Cook food by water.
        Stainless Steel Frying Pan (+ Spatula): Cook food by oil.
        Bowl, Plate, Cup: Hold food / water.
    Cleaner: 
        Surfactant, Sponge / Steel Wool: Including Alkylbenzene Sulfonate C6H5SO3R, Sodium Laureth Sulfate CH3(CH2)11(OCH2CH2)nOSO3Na.
    Material: 
        Acetic Acid CH3COOH: Flavoring.
        Sodium Glutamate COOH(CH2)2-CH(NH2)COONa: Flavoring.
Medical: 
    Hygiene: 
        CH3(CH2)16COONa, Towel: Clean body.
        Toothbrush, Toothpaste (Friction Agent, NaF): Clean teeth.
    Drugs:
        Ethanol C2H6O, KMnO4, I2, H2O2: Kill germs, Disinfect.
        Ibuprofen, Acetaminophen: Painkiller.
        Erythromycin, Amoxicillin, Penicillin, Cephalosporin, Quinols: Kill bacteria, Anti-inflammatory.
    Operation: 
        Face mask, Nitrile gloves: Protection.
        Tweezers, Scalpel: Cut / Hold tissue.
        Syringe: Inject / Extract fluid.
    Hemostasis: 
        Bandage: Stop bleeding.
        Cotton wool: Absorb blood.
Textile: 
    Overnight: 
        Bed: Support body during sleep.
        Mattress: Provide comfortable surface.
        Sleeping Bag: Keep body warm.
        Pillow: Support head.
    Clothes: 
        Underwear (Shirt, Pants): Base layer, Cover body.
        Socks, Gloves, Shoes: Cover feet and hands.
        Hat: Cover head.
        Glasses: Protect eyes.
        Thermal Clothing: Retain body heat.
        Raincoat / Umbrella: Protect from rain.
    Clean: 
        Washing Machine, Washing Powder: Clean clothes.
        Sewing Kit (Needle, Thread): Repair clothes.
Work: 
    Desk, Chair: Provide working environment.
    Measure: 
        Clock: Time.
        Ruler: Length.
        Scale: Weight.
        Thermometer: Temperature.
    Stationery: 
        Paper, Pen: Writing.
        Scissors: Cutting.
        Tape: Binding.
    Miscellaneous Items: 
        ID Card, Documents: Important personal identification. 
        Currency: Medium of exchange.
        Rope: Binding items.
        Bag: Carry items.
